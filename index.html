About

I am a seasoned media and communications professional with over eight years of experience in journalism, broadcasting, digital content creation, and web development. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Science from the University of South Africa (UNISA), and I’ve further strengthened my skill set with certifications in Digital Marketing (Google Digital Garage) and Web Development (Udemy).


My media career began at Tshwane Sun, a Pretoria-based community newspaper, where I worked as a reporter. In 2014, I joined the South African Broadcasting Corporation (SABC) as a radio sports reporter. At SABC, I covered major sports teams, tournaments, and personalities, while also developing key technical skills such as radio scriptwriting and audio editing using tools like Dalet, Audacity, and WavePad.

In 2017 and 2018, I was contracted by LIMAr Media as the lead scriptwriter for broadcast content related to the COSAFA Cup U-17 and senior men’s tournaments, held in Mauritius and South Africa respectively. My work was syndicated across SABC, SuperSport, and other platforms within the COSAFA region.

After leaving SABC in 2018 I spent four months working as a freelance media consultant for Premier Soccer League (PSL) club TS Galaxy, before returning to journalism in 2019 as a freelance reporter for a non-profit news agency GroundUp.

Some of the articles I wrote for GroundUp were published on popular local news websites News24, TimesLive and TheCitizen. Kindly find below some of my published work:

  Furious North West residents stop housing development

In 2019, I took a strategic career break to upskill in digital media. I completed Google’s Digital Marketing course and earned a Web Development certificate from Udemy, equipping me with hands-on skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

Since July 2023, I’ve been managing SizaNazo-365 Sport, a fast-growing sports news YouTube channel with over 12,000 subscribers. The channel delivers up-to-date sports news, in-depth analysis, and highlight packages, and is monetized via the YouTube Partner Program (YPP). Visit SizaNazo-365 Sport

In addition to content creation, I provide freelance graphic design services to small businesses—designing logos, posters, and business profiles using Canva. View Portfolio

I also run a second YouTube channel, Prime Code Media, featuring 60 tutorial videos covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, showcasing my expertise in front-end web development. Visit Prime Code Media
